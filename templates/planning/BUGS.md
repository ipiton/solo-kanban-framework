## Entry Format

```markdown
### [severity][area][estimate] bug-slug

- **Title:** short title
- **Problem:** what is broken
- **Impact:** who or what is affected
- **Fix:** likely direction
- **Refs:** code, issue, task, or log links
- **Status:** open | in-progress | closed
```
